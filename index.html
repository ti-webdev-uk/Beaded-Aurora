About Jayne

A beadaholic
from Lincolnshire

My name is Jayne and I've had a lifelong love of making things. I discovered seed beadweaving in 2007 at a local workshop and never looked back — falling completely in love with the precision, the colour, and the meditative pace of it all.

Over seventeen years I've mastered techniques from peyote stitch to right-angle weave, though my greatest joy remains beaded baubles and home decor. I'm a proud member of The Beadworkers Guild, where I attend a monthly group with fellow enthusiasts.

My shop name was born on a trip to Norway in 2016 — the first time I witnessed the Northern Lights arc across the sky. The colours of that memory live in every piece I make.

"The beautiful dancing lights of the Aurora Borealis will be forever etched in my memory — and the colours of my logo are inspired by them."

Professional Membership

Proud member of
The Beadworkers Guild

The Beadworkers Guild is the UK's leading community for bead artists — dedicated to the craft, education, and advancement of beadwork as an art form. Membership means a commitment to quality and a love of continual learning.

Each month I meet with my local guild group, sharing techniques and inspiration with fellow enthusiasts. That community spirit flows into everything I create and teach.
